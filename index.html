Deterministic AI Infrastructure for Regulated Wealth Platforms

Audit-ready querying of custodian data — designed for platform, aggregator, and custodian deployment.

Kanika is building deterministic AI infrastructure that enables audit-ready access to custodian data in regulated wealth environments. The system translates user intent into constrained, validated queries and automatically generates reproducible evidence and audit trails.

Most AI tools are probabilistic and ship with disclaimers, which makes them unsuitable for use inside compliance, operations, and exam-sensitive workflows. Kanika is designed as a system-level compliance and query layer — not a point solution for individual firms.

Individual RIAs are used as the initial validation wedge to prove workflows in production. Long-term scale comes from platform and custodian deployment, where a single integration reaches hundreds or thousands of downstream advisors.
